What is BDD?
Behaviour Driven Development is TDD, except it provides a new vocabulary and thus focus 
for writing a unit test: it is a feature driven approach to TDD.

This can avoid the how mindset of developers when writing unit tests. 
It's more focused on what something is supposed to do.

So it's just a way to think about writing your tests.
